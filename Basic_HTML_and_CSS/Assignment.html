Strange Creatures of the Deep

The Strangest Fish in the Ocean

From fish with teeth, to fish that glow in the dark, there is no shortage of interesting creatures in the deep. Below is a list of some of the most fascinating, and also terrifying creatures of the deep:

The Blobfish

The Blobfish (native to New Zealand) is one of the worlds most misunderstood fish. With a reputation as the world's ugliest fish, this strange creature is found on display at the Australian Museum's Ichthyology Collection in Sydney.
